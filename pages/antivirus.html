Antivirus & Protection

Modern devices include built‑in protections like firewalls, app store screening, and malware detection. A third‑party security suite can add extras like identity monitoring and VPN. Choose what fits your needs and budget.

  • Built‑in: Minimal cost, low maintenance, good default protection.
  • Third‑party: Added features, but can be heavier and cost a subscription.
  • Mobile devices: Focus on keeping the OS and apps updated; avoid sideloading apps.

Tip: Avoid pop‑up ads claiming your device is infected. Close the tab—don’t click.
